8. Attribute : attribute holo kono entity ke niye kichu information . kono Entity jokhon ami database e rakhbo tokhon sei entityr kichu information amake preserve kore rakhte hoy . jemon products ekta entity . kintu oi products er size, amount , variant etc onk information preserve kore rakhte hoy . setaii attribute . 
Attributes abar 2 prokar: 
- Simple Attribute 
- Composite Attribute

9.  Simple Attribute :
jei attribute ke alada kore r vanga jay na seta Simple Attribute . jemon phone_number, email etc. 

10. Composite Attribute : 
jei attribute ke alada kore onk vabe vanga jay seta Composite Attribute . jemon Name(first_name, last_name, title), address(city, state, country), membership(type, expire_date, start_date) etc

11. Single Valued Attribute :
jei attribute er single value thakbe, multi value hobe na setake Single Valued Attribute bole . jemon user_name, student_id etc . diagram e single valued attribute ke single Oval diye draw kora hoy 


12. Multi Valued Attribute : 
jei attribute er value multiple thakte pare take multi valued attribute bole . jemon phone_number, shipping_address, payment_method etc . diagram e multi valued attribute ke double Oval diye draw kora hoy 

13. Complex Attribute : 
Composite Attribute + MultiValued Attribute = Complex Attribute .
Example : Address (Title, District, Thana, Village, Post, Road) ----- ekhane Address karo karo multi address thakte pare abar prottekta single address abar onk gula attribute e vag korte hoy . tai eta Complex Attribute 


jemon ekta customer er phone, email, name, membership , payment method ai attribute e multiple data thakte pare . jemon name ke venge firstname, lastname ,middlename then shipping address ke venge city, state, country then membership ke venge type, expire_date , start_date etc erokom vabe ekta attribute ke onk vabe vanga jay . egula Composite attribute . abar jodi phone number er kotha boli tahole seta simple o hote pare abar composite o hote pare . 


Attribute 2 Types : 
- Stored : Independent Information, Need to be stored --- Date of Birth
- Derived : Can/Should be calculated from other attribute --- age
